Registering to vote

1. you need to be a member of the cooperative before you are allowed to vote
2. For registration, one needs to have use the ID number that is found on the member list
3. Once one, you can confirm registration by looking at the provided list in the website.

Voting

1. voting will only be done on the day of election
2. the election manager will set the election date
3. voting will be on for a full 24 hours.
4. one can be able to see the total votes of the candidates as voting goes on through the system
5. Winners will be displayed on the home page of the system

candidates

1. one is registred as a candidate by the election manager
2. one needs to be a memeber and a registred voter before becoming a candidate
3. one can be only a candidate for one seat

election manager

1. the election manager controls the whole elaction process
2. He is the one that sets the vpting dates
3. he is the one that updates the ballot sheets with new candidates
4. after election, the super user removes him

Out of controls scenarion

1.In case of a tie, their will be a re run of only the affected candidates
2. in the case of a second re-run, the election manager will break the tie
3.
