Voting Process

voting process involves both registration and the actual voting

Registration

registration simply entails feeding your details to the system and password.
When you open the system, click on the registration link found in the navbar.
This will direct you to the registration page
On the registration page, you willl find a registration form.
Fill in your details into the form and press the register button
N/B make sure you enter your details correctly since once submited no changes can be made.

Voting

On election day once you get to the systems, navigate to the log in page.
log in by entering promoted details
you will be riderected to the voting page.
on the page their will be one ballot paper.
one will be able to vote for their candidates.
you will be able to cast your votes for all the electorates.
on top of that you will have to select your ID number as your voterID
after you cast your vote, you can sign out or proceed to view results.
N/B
in case you are not sure of the candidates that you want to vote for, their is a dedicated button that has a list of all the candidates. the list is arranged according to candidates positions.
